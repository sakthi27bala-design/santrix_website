Key Features

Intelligent • Touchless • Eco

Santrix blends hardware and software to make menstrual care safer, smarter, and sustainable. Below are the core capabilities that define the system.

RFID & Public Modes

Secure registered-user dispensing plus an emergency public-access button—both touch-free.

Inventory & Waste Monitoring

Infrared/ultrasonic sensors track pad stock and bin fill; automated GSM alerts for maintenance.

Solar-Electric Incineration

Solar-backed heater converts used pads into sterile ash; filtration system limits emissions.

Health Vitals Logging

Pulse and temperature capture during access for menstrual health insights (optional & private).

Low-Maintenance Design

Modular enclosure, removable ash tray and easy PCB/service access for field maintenance.

GSM Alerts & Logging

Automatic SMS to staff for restocking and servicing; on-device logs for audit and analytics.

Interactive Dashboard
Monitor pad stock, bin fill, and user vitals in real-time.
Eco Disposal
Solar-assisted incineration ensures safe and odor-free disposal.

Mission

To make menstrual hygiene accessible, dignified, and eco-friendly by combining intelligent automation with a low-power, solar-ready hardware platform.
